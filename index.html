Overview

Lodha Stella

Enjoy luxury living at Lodha Stella - an exclusive residential address in Kapurbawdi, Thane. The project offers 3, 4 & 5 BHK vastu-compliant residencies, with a master bedroom, Duplex, study, & deck areas. A 14000 sq. ft clubhouse is an excellent addition to the project along with a wide range of amenities such as a swimming pool, gym, & indoor games room. Further, 60% of the open space along with a Ganesh Temple beautifies the serene view of the complex.
Located in Kapurbawdi, this project is just 2 minutes away from the Eastern Express Highway. The strategic location of the project allows easy travel times via Ghodbunder Road. Popular schools such as St Joseph's English High School, C.P Goenka School, and Currae Speciality Hospital are within a few minutes' reach. Popular entertainment hangouts such as the Viviana Mall & Korum Mall are close by. With world-class amenities in a prime location - Lodha Stella is the complete residential package!

Lodha Group

Established in 1980, Lodha Group has emerged as one of the premier real estate developers in Mumbai, renowned for its iconic landmarks and contributions to transforming the city's skyline.
